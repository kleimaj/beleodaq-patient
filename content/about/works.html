ABOUT PTCL ABOUT BELEODAQ HOW BELEODAQ WORKS

BELEODAQ is the only HDAC inhibitor FDA approved for the use in patients with relapsed or refractory PTCL1,9

HDAC inhibitors can block the action of HDACs by causing a chemical change to histone proteins. This chemical change can cause DNA to tightly wrap or unwrap, turning on and off genes that affect how T cells grow.3

BELEODAQ can stop cancer cells from growing and cause them to die.1,3
