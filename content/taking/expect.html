BELEODAQ is well tolerated1,2

In clinical trials, most patients had side effects that were generally mild to moderate in severity. The most common side effects were nausea, fatigue, fever, anemia, and vomiting.1,2

Please see the Important Safety Information and full Prescribing Information to review the possible side effects of BELEODAQ.
