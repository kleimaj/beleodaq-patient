BELEODAQ is well tolerated1,2

In clinical trials, most patients had side eﬀects that were generally mild to moderate in severity. The most common side eﬀects were nausea, fatigue, fever, anemia, and vomiting.1,2

Please see the Important Safety Information and full Prescribing Information to review the possible side eﬀects of BELEODAQ.
